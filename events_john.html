Details

2018.4.24 4:10 p.m.–5:20 p.m.
Level 1 Room 146A

Description

In 2016, John announced that he has joined Automattic, the parent company of WordPress.com, WooCommerce, Jetpack, and others with the title, the global head of computational design and inclusion. In this event, John is going to talk about creativity and inclusion as well as how to make inclusive product.

Speaker

John Maeda is an executive, designer, and technologist. He wrote few books on design, technology and business in his earlier years at MIT. He is always passionate at how design, technology and business can merge together. In 2009 he was inducted into the New York Art Director’s Club Hall of Fame, and he received the AIGA Medal in 2010.
