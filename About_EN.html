who we are

ABOUT US

International Family Union (abbr. IFU) is a non-government organization that consists of overseas students to assist international students and their families, especially based on campus security and studying abroad experiences.
In order to provide assistance for parents in emergency, we set up student organizations in various universities and recruit college volunteers periodically.

OUR CULTURE

Sharing, Respect, Creativity
Sharing of our story and experience brings us the international students and families; Sharing of our culture, value, and creed brings us the volunteers; Respect of parents, students and volunteers helps us understand each other; Mutual understanding leads to the choice of creating new possibility!
